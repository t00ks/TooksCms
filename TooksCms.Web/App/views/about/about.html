Welcome to by website, this is a blog / personal project of mine that I use to further my knowledge on all this web development related. Those of you who think I spend more time coding the site than actually writing content would be 100% correct. However the more and more complete the site becomes the more content I am hoping to put up on it. That is until I decided to spend 6 months re-writing the damned thing again!

I am a software developer with almost a decade of experience and in that time if have had a number of jobs in the industry, helped create a number of very large scale business to business web (and other) applications and yet still not finished my own website! But with each and every re-write the site gets that little bit better and so does my working knowledge of bleeding edge web technologies.

I used to run a site called PCRules, the URL www.pcrules.com is now pointing here. This site contained reviews for PC games and hardware, and in its time was fairly popular, for a site that I ran from my bedroom in collage. There was a time that when you searched for Saitek R440 in google that PCRules.com would be the number 1 result. Thanks to not updating the site in years and now moving the URL to point here PCRules is nowhere to be seen. However I have ported all the old PCRules content to this site for history’s sake.
